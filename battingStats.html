Batting Statistics

This page will help calculating some of the basic batting statistics, such as the average & strike rate of a player.

Average

The batting average will be the amount of runs scored by a batter divided by the number of dismissals (via Wikipedia).

Strike Rate

The batting strike rate is the runs scored by the batter over the number of balls, mutliplied by 100 (via Wikipedia).


As an example, I'll refer to the semi-final played at the 2022 Men's T20 World Cup between India and England. Virat Kohli scored 50 runs in 40 balls. So, that would be (50 ÷ 40) * 100 = 125.00.
