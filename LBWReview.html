Reviewing Leg Before Wicket Calls

So, let's get started. Here are the questions you'll be asked when you review an LBW decision:

  1. What was the on-field umpire's decision?
  2. Did the ball pitch in line with the stumps or outside off stump?
  3. Did the ball impact in line with the stumps or outside off stump?
  4. Would the ball have hit the stumps?

Answer these questions to the best of your ability, and at the end, you'll get to know whether the decision stands or is overturned.
